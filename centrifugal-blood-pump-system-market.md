<p><ul><li>Electronic</li><li>Mechanical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The centrifugal blood pump system market comprises two primary types: electronic and mechanical. Electronic centrifugal pumps utilize electrical energy to drive the impeller, offering enhanced control, adaptability, and integration with monitoring systems. They are often preferred for their precision and automated features. In contrast, mechanical centrifugal pumps operate through a manual or fixed mechanism, focusing on simplicity and reliability. These pumps are typically easier to maintain and are widely used in various medical applications, including heart surgeries and long-term support for patients.</p></p>
