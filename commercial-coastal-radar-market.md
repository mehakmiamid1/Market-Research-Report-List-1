<p><strong>The Commercial Coastal Radar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X-Band Radar</li><li>S-Band Radar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial coastal radar market primarily encompasses two main types: X-Band and S-Band radar systems. X-Band radar operates at higher frequencies, providing high-resolution imaging, making it ideal for detecting smaller vessels and detailed coastal features. In contrast, S-Band radar, with its lower frequency, offers longer-range detection and better performance in adverse weather conditions, making it suitable for tracking larger vessels and surveillance over broader areas. Both types are critical for maritime safety, navigation, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
