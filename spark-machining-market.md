<p><ul><li>Sinker EDM</li><li>Wire EDM</li><li>Hole Drilling EDM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spark machining market consists of three main types: Sinker EDM, Wire EDM, and Hole Drilling EDM. Sinker EDM uses a shaped electrode to remove material and create complex geometries. Wire EDM employs a thin, electrically charged wire to cut through metal, allowing for high precision and intricate cuts. Hole Drilling EDM specializes in creating small, precise holes in conductive materials, making it ideal for applications requiring accuracy. Together, these methods cater to diverse industrial machining needs.</p></p>
