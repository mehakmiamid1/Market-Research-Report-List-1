<p><strong>The Belt Drives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ZV</li><li>CV</li><li>CR</li><li>CL</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Belt drives are classified into various market types based on their design and application. ZV (Z-Section V-belts) are used for compact spaces, providing efficient power transmission. CV (C-Section V-belts) offer versatility in moderate-powered applications, ideal for agricultural and industrial uses. CR (Corded Rubber belts) are designed for heavy-duty tasks, ensuring high durability. CL (Cloth belts) are typically utilized in lighter applications, focusing on flexibility and reduced friction. Each type caters to specific mechanical requirements and performance needs.</p></p>
