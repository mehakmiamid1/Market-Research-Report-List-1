<p><p>Aviation Turbine Fuel (ATF) primarily comprises kerosene-type fuels and naphtha-type fuels. Kerosene-type fuels, including Jet A and Jet A-1, are the most widely used, providing high energy density and excellent performance in jet engines, ensuring safety and reliability. Naphtha-type fuels, often used for smaller aircraft and specific applications, have different combustion characteristics and lower freezing points. Both fuel types are vital for the aviation industry, impacting efficiency, environmental standards, and operational costs.</p></p>
