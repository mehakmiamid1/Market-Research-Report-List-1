<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive Chassis Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Suspension ball joints</li><li>Cross-axis joints</li><li>Tie-rods</li><li>Stabilizer Links</li><li>Control arms</li><li>Knuckles and Hubs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive chassis systems market encompasses essential components that ensure vehicle stability and handling. Suspension ball joints connect control arms to the wheel, allowing for smooth movement. Cross-axis joints enable angular motion between components. Tie-rods link the steering to the wheels, facilitating direction changes. Stabilizer links reduce body roll during cornering. Control arms connect the chassis to the wheel assembly, while knuckles and hubs house the wheel bearings, supporting wheel rotation and load distribution. Together, these parts enhance vehicle performance and safety.</p></p>
