<p><p>The Power Liftgate market is primarily divided into two types: Hydraulic Power Liftgates and Electric Power Liftgates. Hydraulic Power Liftgates utilize fluid pressure for lifting and lowering, offering robust performance suitable for heavier loads. They are often found in commercial vehicles. Electric Power Liftgates, on the other hand, rely on electric actuators for operation, providing smoother and quieter functionality. They are commonly used in passenger vehicles for convenience, ease of use, and integration with vehicle electronics, enhancing user experience.</p></p>
