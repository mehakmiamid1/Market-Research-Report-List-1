<p><p>The pet multivitamins market is categorized into fat-soluble and water-soluble vitamins. Fat-soluble vitamins, including A, D, E, and K, are stored in the body’s fatty tissues and are essential for functions such as vision, bone health, and immune support. Water-soluble vitamins, such as B-complex and vitamin C, are not stored in the body and need regular replenishment; they are vital for energy metabolism, skin health, and overall immune function. Both types contribute to pets’ nutritional well-being.</p></p>
