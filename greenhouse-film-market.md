<p><p>The greenhouse film market comprises various types, including Ethylene Vinyl Acetate (EVA), Linear Low-Density Polyethylene (LLDPE), and Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE). EVA is known for its superior clarity, flexibility, and UV resistance, making it ideal for high-performance applications. LLDPE offers excellent tensile strength and puncture resistance, suitable for durable greenhouse covers. LDPE is characterized by its low density and lightness, providing a cost-effective option while offering decent protection. Each type caters to specific agricultural needs and environmental conditions.</p></p>
