<p><strong>The Heart Defibrillators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillator</li><li>External Defibrillator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heart defibrillator market is primarily divided into two types: implantable cardioverter defibrillators (ICDs) and external defibrillators. ICDs are surgically placed inside the body to monitor heart rhythms and deliver shocks when arrhythmias occur, providing long-term management for high-risk patients. In contrast, external defibrillators, including automated external defibrillators (AEDs), are portable devices used in emergency situations to restore normal heart rhythm through electrical shocks, making them essential for immediate response to cardiac emergencies in public settings.</p></p>
