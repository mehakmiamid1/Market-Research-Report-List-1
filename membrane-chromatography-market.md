<p><strong>The Membrane Chromatography Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ion Exchange Membrane Chromatography</li><li>Affinity Film Chromatography</li><li>Hydrophobic Interaction Membrane Chromatography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Membrane chromatography encompasses various techniques, including ion exchange, affinity film, and hydrophobic interaction methods. Ion exchange membrane chromatography utilizes charged membranes to separate biomolecules based on their ionic properties. Affinity film chromatography leverages specific interactions between target molecules and ligands immobilized on membranes, enhancing selectivity. Hydrophobic interaction membrane chromatography separates biomolecules through hydrophobic interactions, effective for purifying proteins based on their surface properties. Together, these methods offer efficient, scalable solutions for downstream processing in biopharmaceuticals and biotechnology.</p></p>
