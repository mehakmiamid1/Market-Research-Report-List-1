<p><p>Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) is a non-invasive imaging technique widely used in medical diagnostics, particularly in ophthalmology, to capture high-resolution cross-sectional images of biological tissues. It employs light waves to take pictures of the retina, allowing healthcare professionals to detect and monitor various eye conditions, such as glaucoma, AMD (Age-related Macular Degeneration), and diabetic retinopathy. The technology is gaining traction in other fields, including cardiology and dermatology, due to its ability to provide detailed imaging without the need for contrast agents.</p><p>The Optical Coherence Tomography Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.2% during the forecast period. <p><p>The Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) market includes various device types catering to different medical applications. Catheter-based OCT devices are utilized for intravascular imaging, providing detailed views of blood vessels. Doppler OCT devices offer enhanced flow measurement capabilities, particularly useful in cardiovascular assessments. Handheld OCT devices are portable, allowing for convenient examinations in diverse settings. Tabletop OCT devices serve as stationary systems, offering high-resolution imaging for diagnostics in ophthalmology and other specialties, thus enhancing clinical decision-making and patient care.</p></p>
