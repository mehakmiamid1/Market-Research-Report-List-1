<p><ul><li>Structured Light Method</li><li>Time of Flight Method (ToF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D Interactive Lens and Assemblies Market features various technologies, including the Structured Light Method and Time of Flight (ToF) Method. The Structured Light Method projects patterns of light onto surfaces, capturing depth information based on distortion to create detailed 3D models. Conversely, the Time of Flight Method measures the time taken for light to travel to an object and back, accurately gauging distance to generate 3D images. Both techniques are vital for applications in gaming, robotics, and augmented reality.</p></p>
