<p><p>The medical disposable gloves market consists of various types, including latex, nitrile, PVC, and others. Latex gloves, made from natural rubber, offer excellent elasticity and tactile sensitivity but may cause allergic reactions in some users. Nitrile gloves, crafted from synthetic rubber, provide superior puncture resistance and durability, making them ideal for sensitive environments. PVC gloves, made from polyvinyl chloride, are economical and suitable for low-risk tasks. Other gloves may include bioplastics or polyethylene variations, catering to diverse applications in healthcare settings.</p></p>
