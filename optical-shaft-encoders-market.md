<p><strong>The Optical Shaft Encoders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Incremental Shaft Encoders</li><li>Absolute Shaft Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical shaft encoders market is primarily categorized into incremental and absolute shaft encoders. Incremental shaft encoders generate output pulses as the shaft rotates, allowing for relative position tracking, ideal for applications needing speed measurement and relative movement. In contrast, absolute shaft encoders provide a unique position value for each shaft angle, offering precise positional information even after power loss. Both types are widely used in automation, robotics, and industrial applications, enhancing the performance and accuracy of motion control systems.</p></p>
