<p><strong>The Monoclonal Antibody Therapy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Human Monoclonal Antibody</li><li>Humanized Monoclonal Antibody</li><li>Chimeric Monoclonal Antibody</li><li>Murine Monoclonal Antibody</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Monoclonal antibody therapy consists of various types, each with distinct characteristics. Human monoclonal antibodies are fully composed of human proteins, minimizing immune response. Humanized monoclonal antibodies are partly human and partly animal, offering improved efficacy and reduced immunogenicity. Chimeric monoclonal antibodies combine both human and murine elements, balancing effectiveness and safety. Murine monoclonal antibodies are entirely derived from mice, typically eliciting stronger immune reactions. These various types cater to different therapeutic needs and market demands, affecting their application in medicine.</p></p>
