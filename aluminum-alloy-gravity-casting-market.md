<p><p>The aluminum alloy gravity casting market encompasses various types, including inclined and fixed gravity casting. Fixed gravity casting features stationary molds, ideal for high-volume production of complex shapes. This market serves sectors like commercial vehicles and passenger cars, where lightweight, durable components are essential. Advancements in casting techniques contribute to improved efficiency and part integrity, driving growth in these automotive applications.</p></p>
