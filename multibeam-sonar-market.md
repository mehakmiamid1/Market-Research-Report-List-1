Low-frequency sonar is ideal for deep-water applications, providing extensive range and coverage but lower resolution. Medium-frequency sonar balances range and resolution, making it suitable for coastal and continental shelf mapping. High-frequency sonar offers superior detail and resolution for shallow waters, often used in port surveys and inspections. Each frequency type serves distinct applications based on depth and required detail.</p></p>
