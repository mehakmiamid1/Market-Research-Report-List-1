<p><p>The Automotive Natural Gas Vehicle (NGV) market consists primarily of two types: CNG (Compressed Natural Gas) vehicles and LNG (Liquefied Natural Gas) vehicles. CNG vehicles are designed for short-range urban use, utilizing compressed natural gas stored in high-pressure tanks, offering cleaner emissions and lower operating costs. In contrast, LNG vehicles are suited for long-haul transportation, featuring liquefied natural gas that allows for greater energy density and range, making them ideal for freight and heavy-duty applications. Both contribute to reducing carbon emissions.</p></p>
