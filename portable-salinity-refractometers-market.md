<p><p>The Portable Salinity Refractometers market comprises two main types: Analog Salinity Refractometers and Digital Salinity Refractometers. Analog refractometers use a curved scale and prism optics to measure salinity by observing light refraction, offering simplicity and durability. Conversely, digital refractometers feature electronic sensors and digital displays, providing more accurate and user-friendly measurements. While analog models are often more affordable and robust for field use, digital variants enhance precision and ease of interpretation, catering to diverse user needs in various applications.</p></p>
