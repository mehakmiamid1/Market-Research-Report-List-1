<p><p>The Agriculture Dyes and Pigments Market is divided into two main types: dyes and pigments. Dyes are colorants that dissolve in a medium, typically used to enhance the appearance of agricultural products and textiles, promoting visual appeal and marketability. Pigments, on the other hand, are finely milled solid colorants that do not dissolve but are used for their opacity and bright coloration in coatings, plastics, and agricultural films, providing protective qualities and enhancing product visibility.</p></p>
