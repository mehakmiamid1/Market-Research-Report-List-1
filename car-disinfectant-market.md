<p><p>The car disinfectant market can be categorized into three types based on efficacy. Efficient disinfectants effectively eliminate a broad spectrum of pathogens, often containing potent active ingredients and offering long-lasting protection. Intermediate disinfectants have moderate effectiveness, suitable for general cleaning but may not eradicate all pathogens. Inefficient disinfectants provide limited protection, typically failing to meet health standards for virus or bacteria elimination and are best for light cleaning rather than sanitizing, posing risks in high-contact areas.</p></p>
