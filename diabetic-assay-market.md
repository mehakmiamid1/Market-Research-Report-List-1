Colorimetric assays employ color change reactions to quantify glucose concentration, offering ease of use and quick results. The "others" category includes emerging technologies and methodologies, such as immunoassays and biosensors, which provide alternative solutions for diabetes monitoring and diagnosis. Each type plays a critical role in enhancing diabetes management.</p></p>
