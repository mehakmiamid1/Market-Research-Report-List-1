Measuring current transformers are designed to provide accurate current readings for monitoring and control in electrical systems, enabling precise load management. In contrast, protective current transformers are used to detect fault conditions and ensure system safety by triggering protective equipment. Both types are crucial for ensuring efficient energy distribution and enhancing the reliability of electrical infrastructure in various applications.</p></p>
