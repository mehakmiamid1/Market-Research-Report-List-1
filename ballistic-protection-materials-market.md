<p><p>The ballistic protection materials market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Poly-Ethylene (PE) Based**: Known for its lightweight and high-strength properties, PE materials offer excellent protection while minimizing bulk, making them ideal for personal armor.</p><p>2. **Aramid (Aromatic Amide) Based**: Aramid fibers, like Kevlar, provide high tensile strength and durability, commonly used in soft armor applications for personal and vehicle protection.</p><p>3. **Ceramic Fabric**: Comprising hard ceramics, this type offers superior resistance to penetration and is often used in composite armor systems for enhancing ballistic performance.</p></p>
