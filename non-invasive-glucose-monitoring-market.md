<p><p>The Non-Invasive Glucose Monitoring market encompasses two main types: Wearable Blood Glucose Monitoring Systems and Table Top Blood Glucose Monitoring Systems. Wearable devices, such as smartwatches or patches, offer continuous monitoring through skin sensors without the need for blood samples, enhancing user convenience.
