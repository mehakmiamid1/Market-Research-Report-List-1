<p><p>Checkweighing systems are crucial in various industries for ensuring product weight compliance. In-motion type systems operate while products are moving on a conveyor, allowing continuous weight monitoring without halting production. This enhances efficiency and throughput. Conversely, intermittent type systems require a pause for weighing, ideal for smaller batches or precise measurements. Both types cater to different operational needs, with in-motion systems focusing on speed and consistency, while intermittent systems prioritize accuracy and detailed inspection.</p></p>
