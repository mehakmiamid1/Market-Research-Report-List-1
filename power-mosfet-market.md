<p><strong>The Power MOSFET Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Depletion Mode Power MOSFET</li><li>Enhancement Mode Power MOSFET</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Power MOSFET market is primarily categorized into two types: depletion mode and enhancement mode Power MOSFETs. Depletion mode MOSFETs are typically used in applications requiring low ON-resistance and faster switching, making them suitable for power management systems. In contrast, enhancement mode MOSFETs, which are more common, are utilized in applications demanding higher efficiency and better control over the conduction state. Both types cater to diverse sectors such as automotive, consumer electronics, and renewable energy, driving market growth.</p></p>
