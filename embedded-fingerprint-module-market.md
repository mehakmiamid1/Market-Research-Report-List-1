<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Embedded Fingerprint Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive Fingerprint Module</li><li>Optical Fingerprint Module</li><li>Ultrasonic Fingerprint Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The embedded fingerprint module market consists of three primary types: capacitive, optical, and ultrasonic fingerprint modules. Capacitive fingerprint modules capture fingerprints by measuring the electrical signals generated by the skin's ridges. Optical fingerprint modules use light to capture images of fingerprints, offering high resolution and clarity. Ultrasonic fingerprint modules utilize sound waves to create detailed 3D images of the fingerprint, often providing enhanced security against spoofing. Each type serves various applications in security, mobile devices, and access control systems.</p></p>
