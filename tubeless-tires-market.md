<p><p>The tubeless tire market can be categorized into two main types: radial tires and bias tires. Radial tires feature fabric plies that run perpendicular to the direction of travel, providing better flexibility, improved fuel efficiency, and enhanced traction. They are ideal for high-speed vehicles. In contrast, bias tires have plies that crisscross at an angle, offering more durability and a stiffer sidewall, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications like trucks and off-road vehicles. Each type serves distinct performance needs.</p></p>
