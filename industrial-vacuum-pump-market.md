<p><strong>The Industrial Vacuum Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Displacement Pumps</li><li>Momentum Transfer Pumps</li><li>Entrapment Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial vacuum pump market encompasses three main types: positive displacement pumps, momentum transfer pumps, and entrapment pumps. Positive displacement pumps create a vacuum by trapping and displacing air, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. Momentum transfer pumps, like rotary vane pumps, utilize kinetic energy to remove air, ideal for low to medium vacuum scenarios. Entrapment pumps capture gas molecules on solid surfaces or in absorbent materials, effective for high-purity applications. Each type serves distinct industrial needs based on performance and application.</p></p>
