<p><ul><li>Static</li><li>Dynamic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>On-board loader scales are categorized into two main market types: static and dynamic. Static scales measure the weight of materials when the loader is stationary, ensuring precise readings and ideal for applications requiring high accuracy. Dynamic scales, on the other hand, measure weight while the loader is in motion, offering real-time data during operations, which increases efficiency and productivity. Each type serves different operational needs, with static scales focusing on accuracy and dynamic scales emphasizing speed and convenience.</p></p>
