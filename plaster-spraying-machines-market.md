<p><strong>The Plaster Spraying Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Sprayer</li><li>Worm-drive Pump Sprayers</li><li>Peristaltic Pump Sprayers</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plaster spraying machines are categorized by their mechanisms. Pneumatic sprayers utilize compressed air to atomize and spray plaster, offering a smooth finish and efficiency in large areas. Worm-drive pump sprayers use a motor-driven screw mechanism to convey plaster, providing consistent flow and pressure. Peristaltic pump sprayers operate by compressing a flexible tube to push material, ensuring minimal waste and easy cleaning. Other market types may include specific custom-built machines or hybrid models tailored for unique applications and materials.</p></p>
