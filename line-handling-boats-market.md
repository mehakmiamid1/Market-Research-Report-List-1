<p><p>Line-Handling Boats are specialized vessels designed for maneuvering and managing mooring lines in marine environments. The market is segmented into two main types: inboard motor and outboard motor boats. Inboard motor boats feature engines housed within the boat's hull, providing enhanced stability and reduced noise, making them suitable for larger operations. Outboard motor boats, on the other hand, have external engines that are easily replaceable and offer greater maneuverability, popular for smaller tasks and coastal operations. Both serve crucial roles in maritime activities.</p></p>
