<p><p>Cetearyl Alcohol (and) Ceteareth-20 is a non-ionic emulsifier and thickener commonly used in cosmetics and personal care products. Cetearyl Alcohol is a fatty alcohol derived from natural sources, while Ceteareth-20 is a polyethylene glycol ether of cetearyl alcohol. Together, they enhance the texture and stability of formulations, enabling effective blending of water and oil phases. This combination is widely utilized in lotions, creams, shampoos, and conditioners for its excellent emollient and skin-conditioning properties.</p><p>The market for Cetearyl Alcohol (and) Ceteareth-20 is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for personal care products formulated with natural ingredients and the rising consumer awareness regarding product safety and efficacy.
