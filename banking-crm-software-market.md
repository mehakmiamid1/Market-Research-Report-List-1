<p><p>Banking CRM software can be categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based solutions are hosted on remote servers, offering flexibility, scalability, and lower upfront costs, making them ideal for banks seeking quick deployment and access from anywhere. Conversely, on-premises solutions are installed locally on a bank's infrastructure, providing greater control over data security and customization, but they often require higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance. Each option suits different banking needs and operational preferences.</p></p>
