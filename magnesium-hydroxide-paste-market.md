<p><p>The Magnesium Hydroxide Paste market can be categorized into two primary production methods: chemical synthesis and physical methods. </p><p>The chemical synthesis method involves the reaction of magnesium salts with alkalis, allowing for precise control over the paste's composition and properties. This approach often yields high purity and consistent quality. </p><p>In contrast, the physical method entails mechanical processes, such as grinding or milling, to create the paste from raw magnesium hydroxide. This method may be less expensive but can lead to variability in quality and characteristics.</p></p>
