<p><p>The instant soups market is categorized into two main types: dried instant soups and condensed instant soups. Dried instant soups are lightweight, shelf-stable products that require rehydration with hot water for preparation, offering convenience and long shelf life. On the other hand, condensed instant soups are thick mixtures that need to be diluted with water or milk before consumption, providing a rich and creamy texture. Both types cater to consumer demand for quick, easy meal solutions while varying in flavor and preparation methods.</p></p>
