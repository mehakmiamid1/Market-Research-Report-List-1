<p><p>The semiconductor encapsulation materials market is divided into two main types: epoxy-based materials and non-epoxy-based materials. Epoxy-based materials are widely used due to their excellent thermal and mechanical properties, offering strong adhesion and protection against environmental factors. Non-epoxy-based materials include silicones, polyimides, and thermoplastics, providing flexibility, higher heat resistance, and lightweight characteristics. Both types serve critical roles in ensuring the durability and reliability of semiconductor devices in various applications, including consumer electronics and automotive systems.</p></p>
