<p><p>The beer chillers market consists of two primary types: coil type and box type. Coil type chillers utilize copper or stainless steel coils that circulate refrigerant, providing efficient cooling as beer flows through the coils. They are often compact and ideal for bars and restaurants. Box type chillers, on the other hand, feature a larger enclosure designed to store multiple kegs or bottles, maintaining a consistent temperature for longer periods. Both types cater to different settings and cooling needs in the beverage industry.</p></p>
