Chemical storage relies on reversible chemical reactions to store energy, providing high energy density and versatility for various applications. Electrochemical storage, commonly found in batteries, stores energy through electrochemical processes, supporting grid stability and renewable integration. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, enhancing efficiency and sustainability.</p></p>
