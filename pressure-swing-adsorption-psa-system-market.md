<p><p>Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA) is a gas separation technology that utilizes adsorbent materials to separate specific gases from a mixture under varying pressure conditions. In a PSA system, gases are adsorbed onto solid adsorbents at high pressure, allowing the desired gas to be captured while unwanted components are released. When the pressure is lowered, the adsorbed gases desorb, allowing for their collection. PSA systems are widely used in industries such as natural gas processing, oxygen generation, and hydrogen production.</p><p>The PSA system market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for efficient gas separation technologies and rising industrial applications. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.6% during the forecast period. Key trends include advancements in adsorbent materials, integration of automation technology for enhanced efficiency, and a growing focus on sustainable energy solutions such as hydrogen production. Additionally, the rising emphasis on environmental regulations and the need for cleaner technologies in industries are propelling market demand. As industries seek to improve process efficiency and reduce operational costs, PSA systems are becoming increasingly vital in various applications across several sectors.</p></p>
