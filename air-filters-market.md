<p><p>The air filters market includes several types designed for various filtration needs. Pre-filters capture larger particles to extend the life of more efficient filters. Sub-HEPA filters provide moderate filtration for less demanding applications. Secondary filters enhance overall air quality by trapping medium-sized particles. HEPA filters are highly effective, capturing 99.97% of particles down to 0.3 microns, making them ideal for healthcare and cleanroom environments. ULPA filters offer even higher efficiency, capturing 99.999% of particles, suitable for stringent air quality requirements.</p></p>
