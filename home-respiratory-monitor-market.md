<p><p>The home respiratory monitor market includes various types of devices designed to enhance patient care and management of respiratory conditions. Pulse oximeters measure oxygen saturation levels in the blood, aiding in monitoring respiratory health. Incentive spirometers encourage deep breathing to improve lung function and prevent complications. Peak flow meters assess lung function by measuring air flow rate, helping in asthma management. Additionally, other devices may include nebulizers and respiratory sound monitors, providing comprehensive assessment and support for patients at home.</p></p>
