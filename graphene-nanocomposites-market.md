<p><p>Graphene nanocomposites encompass various types, including Graphene Oxide (GO), Graphene Nano Platelets (GNP), and Graphene Polymer composites. Graphene Oxide, characterized by its oxygenated functional groups, is widely utilized for enhanced dispersion in solvents and polymers, boosting mechanical properties. Graphene Nano Platelets consist of thin layers of graphene, improving conductivity and strength in various applications. Graphene Polymer composites combine polymers with graphene materials, resulting in superior thermal, electrical, and mechanical properties, catering to diverse fields such as electronics, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
