<p><p>The singlet lenses market comprises various types, each serving distinct optical needs. Plano-convex lenses, with one flat and one convex surface, are ideal for focusing light. Plano-concave lenses, having one flat and one concave surface, diverge light rays. Biconvex lenses feature two outwardly curved surfaces for convergence, while double concave lenses are curved inward on both sides, enhancing divergence. Additionally, the market includes specialized types like aspheric and cylindrical lenses, catering to advanced optical applications in industries such as photography, eyewear, and laser technology.</p></p>
