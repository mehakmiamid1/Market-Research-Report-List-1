<p><p>Refrigerated air dryers are essential in removing moisture from compressed air systems. The market is primarily divided into cycling and non-cycling types. Cycling refrigerated air dryers adjust their cooling capacity based on the demand, providing energy efficiency by reducing power consumption during low-load periods. Non-cycling dryers, on the other hand, operate continuously at a fixed capacity, ensuring consistent performance but potentially leading to higher energy use. Both types serve different applications based on efficiency and operational requirements.</p></p>
